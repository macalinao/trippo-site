Trippo

Trips tailored to your personality

Know yourself, know your worth

Using personality info derived from your Facebook and IBM Watson, Trippo suggests the best places to travel to.

Back to back trip itinerary

Have you ever though, Oh man oh man, not again while planning? Trippo decides where to go at your destination so you can spend less time planning and more time doing.

We use information about activities from Expedia and pipe it through IBM Watson to determine how well an activity matches with your personality.

Ever since you left your city

We know that navigation has a reputation for being annoying if you're not familiar with the city. Using the ESRI api, we have maps to let you know where to go.

You know when that hotline bling

Using RingCentral, we send you periodic text messages whenever prices drop for your trip, so you can know when it's a good time to buy.
